Welcome to the Yoga Loft in Plymouth, Devon!

The studio has been carefully designed to offer a tranquil space in which to take off your shoes and enjoy the practice of yoga. 'A beautiful practice deserves a beautiful space' has been the mantra behind the creation of the studio.

We offer a full timetable of daily classes in a range of yoga styles. We have classes for all levels of experience: from complete beginners to more experienced practitioners.

We look forward to seeing you soon. Namaste
