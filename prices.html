Pricing and online purchase policy

Timetabled class passes

  • All timetabled class pass sessions bought online, never expire and are valid until used. There is no time limit.
  • Passes can be used for any class with the same, or shorter, session length. They are not tied to a specific class.
  • You may also buy passes to give away to your friends or family (ask at the studio, to have passes transfered to someone else).
  • If pass prices go up in the future, we will continue to accept passes purchased previously at the lower price.

Workshop tickets and one-on-one class passes

  • Workshop passes are non-refundable when cancelled within 30 days of the event, unless the workshop is cancelled or rescheduled. A lot of work goes into the preparation of workshops and they are usually run on the basis of a minimum attendance. If you book and later change your mind, you won't receive a refund.
  • One-on-one personalised class passes are non-refundable when cancelled within 7 days of the booked time slot, unless your instructor cancels or reschedules. If you book a one-on-one session, your instructor will allocate some of their time specifically for you. They can't make other plans for the period of time you have booked, so we think it's only fair to compensate them if you cancel or change your mind. If you book and pay for a one-on-one session and we are unable to schedule your preferred instructor at your requested time, you will, of course, be offered a full refund.
  • Refunds for cancellations under extenuating circumstances will be decided on a case by case basis at the discretion of the Yoga Loft.

Concessions

We offer discounted rates for:

  • Students
  • Seniors
  • Services Personnel (past or present)
  • NHS Staff & Care Workers

On your first visit, we may ask to see ID showing your entitlement and make a note on your account. On subsequent visits, there is no need to show ID. Just select the discount passes in the payment form.

If you need a concession for reasons not listed here, just ask. All requests will receive due consideration.

There is also a Monday Community Class, where admission is donation based. You may give whatever you can afford, attend free of charge if you need to, or make a contribution that isn't money, like making a "pay it forward" donation of your time to a charitable cause or doing something heroic for someone who needs you.

Freestyle

Freestyle passes are used for personal practice time at the loft. They cover a pre-booked, pre-paid, personal practice session of up to 2 hours without an instructor present or with a teacher who is in training.
